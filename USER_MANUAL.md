## Introduction

**Pawthos** is a comprehensive veterinary management system designed for the City Veterinary Office of San Pedro. This web-based application streamlines veterinary operations, record-keeping, and administrative tasks for efficient animal healthcare management.

### Key Features
- **Comprehensive Record Management**: Track vaccinations, medical records, animal control, meat inspection, and reproductive records
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Manage veterinary appointments and service requests
- **Pain Assessment Tools**: Monitor and assess animal pain levels
- **Dashboard Analytics**: View real-time statistics and reports
- **User Management**: Control access and permissions for different user types
- **Notification System**: Stay updated with alerts and reports

## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- **Web Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Internet Connection**: Stable internet connection required
- **Screen Resolution**: Minimum 1024x768 recommended

### Accessing the System
1. Open your web browser and navigate to the Pawthos system URL
2. You will be redirected to the login page
3. Enter your credentials (email and password)
4. Click "Sign In" to access the system

### First-Time Login
If this is your first time logging in:
1. You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) via email
2. Enter the OTP code to verify your account
3. You may be prompted to change your password for security
4. Complete the setup process to access the dashboard

## Record Management

The Records section is the core of the Pawthos system, managing all veterinary and administrative records.

### Available Record Types

#### 1. Vaccination Records
**Purpose**: Track all animal vaccinations
- **Information Tracked**:
  - Animal details (name, species, age, gender)
  - Owner information
  - Vaccine type and batch number
  - Vaccination date and veterinarian
  - Next due date for boosters
- **Features**:
  - Add new vaccination records
  - Search and filter existing records
  - Export data for reporting
  - Track vaccination schedules

#### 2. Medical Records
**Purpose**: Comprehensive health information
- **Information Tracked**:
  - Diagnosis and treatment history
  - Medications prescribed
  - Clinical notes and observations
  - Lab results and test data
  - Surgical procedures
- **Features**:
  - Detailed medical history
  - Treatment tracking
  - Medication management
  - Progress monitoring

#### 3. Animal Control Records
**Purpose**: Log animal seizure activities
- **Information Tracked**:
  - Capture date and location
  - Species and animal details
  - Reason for capture (rabies suspect, etc.)
  - Disposition and outcome
  - Officer information
- **Features**:
  - Location tracking
  - Species categorization
  - Outcome reporting
  - Statistical analysis

#### 4. Meat Inspection Records
**Purpose**: Document meat product inspections
- **Information Tracked**:
  - Inspection date and inspector
  - Product details and source
  - Inspection results
  - Compliance status
  - Quality standards met
- **Features**:
  - Quality assurance tracking
  - Compliance monitoring
  - Inspection scheduling
  - Report generation

#### 5. Vet Health Certificate (Shipping Permits)
**Purpose**: Manage animal transport permits
- **Information Tracked**:
  - Origin and destination
  - Animal details and health status
  - Permit validity dates
  - Compliance documentation
  - Transport conditions
- **Features**:
  - Permit generation
  - Validity tracking
  - Compliance monitoring
  - Export documentation

#### 6. Pet Sterilization Records (Reproductive Records)
**Purpose**: Manage breeding and sterilization
- **Information Tracked**:
  - Breeding history
  - Estrus cycles
  - Pregnancy records
  - Whelping/kidding details
  - Sterilization procedures
- **Features**:
  - Breeding management
  - Cycle tracking
  - Birth records
  - Population control

### Record Management Features

#### Common Operations
- **Add New Record**: Click the "Add New" button in each section
- **Search Records**: Use the search bar to find specific records
- **Filter Data**: Apply filters by date, type, or status
- **Edit Records**: Click on any record to modify information

- **Complete Information**: Fill all required fields

## Pain Assessment System

The Pain Assessment tool helps monitor and evaluate animal pain levels for better treatment outcomes.

### Pain Assessment Features

#### Assessment Process
1. **Select Animal**: Choose the pet for assessment
2. **Evaluate Pain Level**: Use standardized pain scales
3. **Record Observations**: Document behavioral indicators
4. **Add Notes**: Include detailed observations
5. **Save Assessment**: Store for future reference

#### Pain Level Indicators
- **Mild (1-3)**: Slight discomfort, minimal behavioral changes
- **Moderate (4-6)**: Noticeable pain, some behavioral changes
- **Severe (7-8)**: Significant pain, obvious distress
- **Extreme (9-10)**: Intense pain, severe behavioral changes

#### Assessment Categories
- **Behavioral Indicators**: Activity level, appetite, vocalization
- **Physical Signs**: Posture, movement, response to touch
- **Clinical Observations**: Vital signs, medical indicators
- **Owner Reports**: Pet owner observations and concerns

### Pain Assessment Management
- **Create Assessments**: Add new pain evaluations
- **View History**: Track pain levels over time
- **Update Records**: Modify existing assessments
- **Generate Reports**: Analyze pain management effectiveness
- **Treatment Planning**: Use assessments for treatment decisions

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Problems
**Issue**: Cannot log in to the system
**Solutions**:
- Verify email and password are correct
- Check if account is activated (OTP confirmation)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different web browser
- Contact system administrator

**Issue**: OTP not received
**Solutions**:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Wait a few minutes for delivery
- Request new OTP code
- Contact IT support

#### Performance Issues
**Issue**: System running slowly
**Solutions**:
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Restart web browser

**Issue**: Pages not loading properly
**Solutions**:
- Disable browser extensions
- Update web browser
- Check JavaScript is enabled
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Contact technical support

#### Data Issues
**Issue**: Records not saving
**Solutions**:
- Check all required fields are filled
- Verify internet connection
- Try saving again
- Refresh page and retry
- Contact system administrator

**Issue**: Search not working
**Solutions**:
- Clear search terms and try again
- Check spelling of search terms
- Use different search criteria
- Refresh the page
- Contact support if persistent

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What is Pawthos?**
A: Pawthos is a comprehensive veterinary management system designed for the City Veterinary Office of San Pedro to manage animal records, appointments, and administrative tasks.

**Q: Who can use the system?**
A: The system is designed for veterinary staff, administrators, and authorized personnel of the City Veterinary Office.

**Q: Is the system secure?**
A: Yes, the system uses secure authentication, encrypted data transmission, and role-based access control to protect sensitive information.

### Account and Access

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Contact your system administrator to create an account. You will receive an email with activation instructions.

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Contact your system administrator to reset your password. You will receive a new temporary password via email.

**Q: Can I change my role or permissions?**
A: Only system administrators can change user roles and permissions. Contact your admin for role changes.

### Data Management

**Q: How do I backup my data?**
A: The system automatically backs up data. You can also export records using the export features in each section.

**Q: Can I import data from other systems?**
A: Contact your system administrator for data import procedures and supported formats.

**Q: How long is data retained?**
A: Data retention policies are managed by system administrators. Contact them for specific retention periods.

### Technical Support

**Q: Who do I contact for technical support?**
A: Contact your system administrator or IT support team for technical issues.

**Q: What information should I provide when reporting issues?**
A: Include your username, browser type and version, error messages, and steps to reproduce the issue.

**Q: Are there training materials available?**
A: This user manual provides comprehensive guidance. Additional training may be available through your administrator.

### System Features

**Q: Can I access the system from mobile devices?**
A: Yes, the system is responsive and can be accessed from tablets and smartphones, though desktop is recommended for full functionality.

**Q: How often is the system updated?**
A: System updates are managed by administrators. You will be notified of any significant changes or downtime.

**Q: Can I customize the dashboard?**
A: Dashboard customization options are limited. Contact your administrator for available customization options.
